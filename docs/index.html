What is X-Ray Selection Tools?

Gumroad | BlenderArtists

This addon adds new box, circle and lasso selection tools that can automatically toggle x-ray shading, perform selection through with or without x-ray shading, select objects either inside or overlapping a selection area, change selection settings by changing drag direction of selection.
